POSITRON
We are here to help you start a conversation
           about mental health
In physics, a Positron is the opposite of a negative charge.
           It can annihilate negative particles (electrons) with a burst of energy.
We want do our part in making mental health accessible to everyone,
           not only to those with the resources. Mental health shouldn’t be just a luxury.
Download our app and become a part of the mental health movement
         for yourself, or someone else. We can help you get a conversation started,
                     show you resources in your area, and track your daily moods and wellbeing.
